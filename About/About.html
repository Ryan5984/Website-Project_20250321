ABOUT QA



The Quality Assurance (QA) Department is integral to the implementation and maintenance of a Quality Management System (QMS) based on ISO 9000:2015 and its quality management principles. We ensure that processes are designed and controlled to consistently meet customer and legal requirements, and organizational objectives. By emphasizing process efficiency, evidence-based decision-making, and continual improvement, we help the organization enhance product and service quality, minimize risks, and drive operational excellence.


Guided by the QMS principles, the QA Department fosters a culture of customer focus, leadership, engagement of people, and relationship management. We ensure that the quality policy and quality objectives to are effectively communicated and integrated into our business operations. Through a systematic approach quality assurance, including audits, corrective actions, and performance monitoring, QA supports the organization's commitment to continuous improvement and long-term success.
